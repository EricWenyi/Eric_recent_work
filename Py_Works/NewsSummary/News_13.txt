 6 January 2017 Last updated at 07:46 GMT  A team of British doctors led by Dr Rola Hallam and journalist Paul Conroy has travelled to the Syria/Turkey border with a convoy filled with medical supplies. Aid workers plan to use the equipment to set up a children's hospital near Aleppo. Rola Hallam made a video blog of the journey. She arrived at the border on 23 December. The lorry arrived a few days later, on 2 January.

Bangalore sex attacks: CCTV captures horror on 1 January - BBC News 5 January 2017 Last updated at 16:59 GMT  Police in southern India say there is no evidence of mass sex attacks during new year celebrations in central Bangalore, despite a number of women telling the media they had been assaulted by groups of men. CCTV footage of one violent attack in the early hours of 1 January elsewhere in the city has come to light, with four men arrested over the incident. Filmed and edited by Jaltson AC.

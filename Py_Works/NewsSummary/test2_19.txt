BBC Sound of 2017: Rag 'N' Bone Man - BBC News 5 January 2017 Last updated at 10:42 GMT  Rag 'N' Bone Man has come second on the BBC's Sound of 2017, which aims to predict the biggest new acts of the coming year. Born Rory Graham, the blues singer rose to recognition in 2016 when his track Human, made the UK top 10 and reached number one in Germany, Belgium, Austria and Switzerland.
